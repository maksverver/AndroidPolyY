Privacy policy for Poly-Y for Android, effective from July 16, 2024.

Poly-Y (henceforth, “the app”) is an offline app that only stores information
on the device on which it is installed.

The app stores the following data:

  * the current game in progress
  * progress made in campaign mode

The user may delete the stored data at any time, by clearing the app data, or by
uninstalling the app.

The app:

  * does not collect personal information
  * does not collect biometric information
  * does not collect location information
  * does not contain ads
  * does not support accounts
  * does not integrate with online services
  * does not integrate with third-party apps

For more information, email the author (Maks Verver) at support@verver.ch.
